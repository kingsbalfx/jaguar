## TIME ALLOCATION

### NEW SYSTEM (Daily+4HR Brief + H1→M15→M5→M1 Execution)
```
24HR Brief:    1 minute (Daily chart - macro picture)
4HR Brief:     1-2 minutes (Support context, FVG zones, OB levels)
H1 Analysis:   2-3 minutes (Liquidity on H1, price action)
M15 Deep Work: 8-10 minutes (WHERE THE REAL WORK HAPPENS)
M5 Entry:      2-3 minutes (Wait for setup)
M1 Execute:    1 minute (Take position)
─────────────────────────────────

```

**Key:** Brief (Daily+4HR) eliminates bad setups BEFORE you waste time on H1→M15→M5→M1

---

## STEP 1: DAILY + 4HR BRIEF CHECK (2-3 MIN)

**This is your CONTEXT filter - do this first**

### A) 24HR (Daily) Chart - 1 minute

**What to scan:**
```
✓ Macro Structure: HH/HL (up) or LL/LH (down)?
✓ Liquidity Levels: Where have prices swept recently?
✓ Order Blocks: From major moves (visible on daily)?
✓ Volume Balance: Any extreme imbalance?
✓ Price Action: Rejection candles? Engulfing patterns?
✓ Overall Bias: Bullish or bearish daily trend?
```

**What NOT to do:**
```
✗ Don't analyze individual candles deeply
✗ Don't look for micro reversals
✗ Don't waste time here - it's a glance
```

**Output:** "Daily is bullish HH/HL, liquidity at X, OBs at Y, volume balanced → Continue to 4HR"

---

### B) 4HR Chart - 1-2 minutes

**What to scan:**
```
✓ Recent Swings: Highs/lows from last 2-3 days
✓ Liquidity Events: Where price has swept?
✓ FVG Zones: Fair value gaps that need filling?
✓ Order Blocks: From recent sweep moves?
✓ Volume: Any extreme imbalance visible?
✓ Price Action: any rejection candles, stops run above/below FVGs?
✓ Bias Alignment: Does 4HR agree with daily?
```

**Your goal:** Build a "liquidity map" - zones where price is likely to visit

**Output:** "4HR confirms bullish, FVG at X (needs filling), OB at Y, sweep at Z → Setup possible in these zones"

---

### DAILY + 4HR = CONTEXT FILTER

If **24HR + 4HR agree** (both bullish or both bearish):
```
✅ CONTINUE to H1 analysis
```

If they **DISAGREE** (one up, one down):
```
❌ SKIP - Don't trade until alignment
```

If **4HR structure is messy** (no clear direction, mixed signals):
```
❌ SKIP - Wait for clarity
```

---

## STEP 2: H1 ANALYSIS (2-3 MIN)

**Quick H1 scan after 24HR+4HR approval**

**What to check:**
```
✓ H1 Bias: Does it agree with daily+4HR?
✓ Recent Swings: H1's own structure
✓ Liquidity: Where H1 might sweep?
✓ FVG: Any H1-level FVGs related to 4HR zones?
✓ Price Action: Any rejection candles on H1?
```

**Output:** "H1 is bullish, aligns with 4HR, liquidity at X, ready for M15"

---

## STEP 3: M15 DEEP WORK (8-10 MIN)

**This is where 80% of your real analysis happens**

**What to find:**
```
✓ M15 Swing Structure: HH/HL (up) or LL/LH (down)?
✓ Trend: Is there acceleration, deceleration, or consolidation?
✓ Liquidity Zones: Where will M15 sweep?
✓ Order Blocks: From recent M15 moves
✓ FVGs: Fair value gaps on M15 timeframe
✓ Volume: Any extreme imbalance on M15?
✓ Reversal Candles: Rejection candles at key levels?
✓ Moving Averages: EMA 200 bias?
✓ Where is the next logical sweep?
```

**Your goal:** Identify the EXACT zone where a setup will form

**Output:** "M15 shows bullish structure, next LL needs testing at X, OB at Y, FVG at Z → Setup ready for M5"

---

## STEP 4: M5 ENTRY CONFIRMATION (2-3 MIN)

**Wait for the M5 setup to form**

**What to check:**
```
✓ Price Action: Rejection candle at expected zone?
✓ FVG: Any M5-level FVG confirming entry?
✓ Volume: Momentum on the rejection candle?
✓ Momentum: Is price moving in the right direction?
```

**Your job:** Don't force entries - wait for M5 to show the signal

**Output:** "M5 shows rejection at expected zone, FVG confirmed, momentum up → Ready for M1"

---

## STEP 5: M1 EXECUTION (1 MIN)

**Take the position**

**What to check:**
```
✓ Break: Does it break above/below the entry?
✓ Momentum: Strong momentum confirmation?
✓ Fill: Can I get a good fill?
```

**Output:** Take long position. SL below setup zone. TP at 4HR/daily reference level.

```

         ↓
```

- Daily: 1 min
- 4HR: 1-2 min
- H1: 2-3 min
- M15: 8-10 min
- M5: 2-3 min (mostly waiting)
- M1: 1 min (execute)
- **TOTAL: ~15-20 minutes**

## CRITICAL RULES

### ✅ DO:

1. **Always check 24HR + 4HR first** - These filter out 90% of bad setups
2. **Look for FVGs, not just S/R** - FVGs are where setups form
3. **Identify sweep zones early** - Find where liquidity pools are on 4HR
4. **Let M15 do the work** - Spend 8-10 minutes here finding the exact setup
5. **Wait for M5 rejection** - Don't enter early, wait for confirmation
6. **Execute on M1** - Fast and clean, target already identified

### ❌ DON'T:

1. **Skip the 24HR + 4HR context** - You'll waste time on bad setups
2. **Look for S/R levels only** - Include FVGs, OBs, volume balance, price action
3. **Spend 15 minutes on 24HR + 4HR** - Should be 2-3 minutes max
4. **Trade if 24HR + 4HR disagree** - Wait for alignment
5. **Analyze 5+ FVGs on M15** - Focus on 2-3 most relevant zones
6. **Chase M1 entries** - If setup doesn't confirm, skip and wait for next
